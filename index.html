Why Travel Times

No clutter. No distractions. Just the information you need to make the right route choice.

Built for commuters

Everything you need. Nothing you don't.

Hyperlocal traffic intelligence, stripped down to the signal.

  • Real-time travel times

    Live updates for every major freeway — so you can make the smart call before you even start the car.

  • Color-coded status

    Green, yellow, red. Pick the fastest route in seconds without parsing a single number.

  • Smart & scheduled alerts

    Delay alerts when traffic turns — plus scheduled notifications for the times that matter most. Built for daily commutes.

  • Active incidents

    Road closures, roadwork, and crashes — grouped by severity, right inside route details. Know what’s blocking you before you go.

  • Favorites, everywhere

    Pin your go-to routes for a personalized dashboard. iCloud syncs your picks across iPhone, iPad, and Apple Watch.

  • Share in one tap

    Send current travel times and delay info to anyone — iMessage, Mail, or any share destination. Perfect for running late.

  • Redesigned route details

    Horizontal stat cards, estimated arrival, relative timestamps, and delay indicators tuned to severity — at a single glance.

  • Sort & search

    Find routes by name, direction, or destination. The whole freeway network in a single tap.

  • Official WisDOT data

    Every metric comes directly from Wisconsin DOT. No guesswork. No crowdsourcing. No nonsense.
