APPENDIX A — PRIVACY POLICY

Your commute data stays yours.

Travel Times Milwaukee is built without trackers or personal data collection. Here is how we keep it that way.

LAST UPDATED — JANUARY 2025

DOC A — PRIVACY POLICYSHEET A · TRAVELTIMESAPP.COM
A.1

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Travel Times Milwaukee ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application and website.

A.2

Information we collect

Travel Times Milwaukee does not collect any personal data from users. Our app and website are designed to provide traffic information without requiring any personal information.

A.3

Data collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, including but not limited to:

  • Personal identification information (name, email address, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Device information
  • Usage analytics
  • Cookies or tracking data
A.4

Traffic information

The traffic and travel times information provided by our app comes from the Wisconsin Department of Transportation. We do not collect or store this data ourselves.

A.5

Third-party services

Our app is distributed through the Apple App Store, which may collect standard analytics data as part of their service. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for information about their data collection practices.

A.6

Website analytics

Our website may use standard web server logs that collect anonymous information such as IP addresses, browser types, and pages visited. This information is used solely for website maintenance and performance optimization.

A.7

Children's privacy

Our app and website are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

A.8

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact
